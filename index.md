# Larpable

> Detect fake entrepreneurs. Protect yourself.

If you have scrolled entrepreneur-Twitter or the TikTok 'founder' side for more than ten minutes, you have seen it: someone claiming $2M ARR with a screenshot that doesn't even match the currency, someone with 50k followers and zero shipped product, someone whose 'viral thread' was bought. Larpable is an educational catalog of these patterns with real examples and verification checklists. It does not name and shame, it teaches you to read the patterns.
