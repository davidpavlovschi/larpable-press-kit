# Overview of Larpable

> Detect fake entrepreneurs. Protect yourself.

The creator economy has a fraud problem: fabricated revenue screenshots, borrowed social proof, engineered viral threads, and personal brands built on zero actual shipped product. Larpable is the educational platform that catalogs every trick in the book, walks through real examples of the patterns in action, and gives you a checklist for evaluating any founder claim before you trust it, invest in it, or buy their course.
